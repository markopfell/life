SpaceX was founded under the belief that a future where humanity is out exploring the stars is fundamentally more exciting than one where we are not. Today SpaceX is actively developing the technologies to make this possible, with the ultimate goal of enabling human life on Mars.

RF System Engineer

Responsibilities:
• Design and development of RF Systems for spacecraft, launch vehicles, and ground stations.
• Development of link budgets for spacecraft TT&C, proximity links and re-radation systems.
• Write or update acceptance and qualification test procedures for radio related space qualified avionics components.
• Debug and characterize RF components and subsystems used for spacecraft communications in L-band, S-band and Ku-band.
• Development of automated test equipment and procedures to allow production testing of RF avionics components and subsystems.
• Assist in bring up, integration and troubleshooting of communication systems. Work closely with system integration and flight software teams during hardware in the loop testing.
• Supporting RF system bring up and verification at launch sites.

Basic Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree or higher in an engineering discipline with emphasis in electromagnetic theory or RF communication system.
• Must have at least 3 years of professional experience in RF System engineering.
• Must have experience with RF impairments analysis and link budget analysis.
• Experience with Radio related test equipment such as spectrum analyzers and Network analyzers.
• Must be able to navigate the smith chart and experience with transmission line theory, impedance matching, filter theory and S-parameters.
• Must have experience with RF passive components such as Hybrid couplers, Wilkinson dividers, waveguide, circulators, directional couplers, and antennas.
• Experience with digital communication theory with emphasis on Bit Error Rate, EVM and System Noise Figure calculation.

Preferred Skills and Experience:
• Experience in design of space communication systems and familiarity with space qualified RF electronic design.
• Previous experience with RF Design tools such ADS, AWR Microwave Office.
• Matlab, Python and/or LabView programming experience.
• Familiarity with electromagnetic propagation and antenna systems.
• Familiarity with MIL-STD-461F, SMC-S-016, and NTIA standards.
• Experience characterizing radio performance in C-Band, X-Band and Ka-Band frequencies.
• Experience performing interference analysis and EMI/EMC evaluation for RF systems.
• Knowledge of digital communication systems, spread spectrum, and modulation types such as FSK, BPSK, QPSK, QAM, and PCM/FM.
• Excellent communication skills, documentation skills and proven track record of being a team player.

Additional Requirements:
• Must be able to work extended hours and weekends as needed.
